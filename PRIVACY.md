# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

## Overview

NeuroFit is a client-side web application. Your workout data, body composition data, and settings are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and are never transmitted to any server operated by the developers of this project.

## Local Data Storage

All data you enter into the app (workouts, weight logs, body measurements, settings, and preferences) is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data:

- Never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it
- Is not collected, transmitted, or accessible by the app developers
- Can be deleted at any time by clearing your browser data
- Should be backed up regularly using the Export feature in Settings, as localStorage is not a persistent database

## AI Coach Feature

If you enable the AI Coach feature, your data is transmitted directly to the third-party AI provider you select in Settings (e.g., Anthropic, Google, OpenRouter, or other supported providers). This data may include workout history, exercise details, and any information you include in your patient/user profile.

**This data is subject to the privacy policy of the AI provider you choose.** The developers of NeuroFit do not intermediate, store, or have access to your AI Coach conversations. You are responsible for reviewing and accepting the privacy policy of your selected AI provider before enabling this feature.

API keys you enter are stored in your browser's localStorage and are sent directly to the selected provider. They are never transmitted to the app developers.

## No Analytics or Tracking

NeuroFit does not include any analytics, tracking scripts, cookies, or telemetry. The app makes no network requests except those explicitly initiated by you (AI Coach queries, YouTube video links).

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
